Common questions

How long is an SIA licence valid for?

Most SIA licences are valid for 3 years from the date of issue.

How early should I apply to renew?

The SIA recommends applying at least 16 weeks (4 months) before your licence expires. Processing times have extended to 12–16 weeks in recent years, so applying at the 16-week mark is now the minimum safe window.

What happens if my licence expires while a renewal is being processed?

If you applied before your licence expired and the SIA is still processing the renewal, you may continue working in a licensable role. Your employer must verify this. It is always safer to apply well in advance.

Do I need refresher training before renewing?

Yes. From April 2026, close protection operatives must complete refresher training to renew their licence. Requirements vary by licence type — check the latest SIA guidance or use Wren to get alerts when requirements change.

Can I check my renewal application status online?

Yes. You can check your application status on the SIA website using your applicant reference number.
