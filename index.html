What the service does

SIA Watch monitors the official pages your team would otherwise need to check by hand, then sends a clear alert when something changes that could affect staffing, training or licence renewals.

01

Clear alerts

The feed stays quiet until something changes in a way that could affect licensing, training or licence status.

02

Source-linked evidence

Each alert includes the source page, extracted lines and update signal so someone in ops can verify it quickly.

03

Already running

The monitor is live, scheduled and stateful. It keeps the baseline, compares new fetches and only delivers when it finds a real change.

How the service works

The service checks the official pages, compares them with the last saved version, summarises the change and sends the result into email, Telegram or an internal feed.

1

Track

Follow the GOV.UK and SIA pages tied to training, renewals and licence checks.

2

Compare

Compare each fetch to the last stored version and ignore pages that did not materially change.

3

Summarise

Extract the lines that matter and reduce them into a short operational update.

4

Alert

Send the result into email, Telegram or an internal feed and keep a record of what changed.
